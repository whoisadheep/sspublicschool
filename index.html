About our School

Admission Process

Join our Learning Community

We welcome applications from students who demonstrate academic potential and a commitment to our school values. Our admission process is designed to be transparent and fair.

Steps for Admission:

  1. Submit the online application form
  2. Attend the entrance assessment (for applicable grades)
  3. Participate in the interaction session
  4. Receive admission decision
  5. Complete enrollment formalities

Required Documents:

  • Birth Certificate (original + photocopy)
  • Two Passport size photographs
  • One family photograph (postcard size)
  • Aadhaar Card (photocopy) of both child and parents
  • Previous school report cards (if applicable)
  • Transfer Certificate (for grades 2 and above)
  • UDISE Code of last school
  • AAPAR ID if generated in the last school
